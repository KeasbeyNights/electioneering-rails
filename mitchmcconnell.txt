Abortion Mitch McConnell  voted in favor of the amendment to prevent aid funds from being used for coercive abortions.

Education Mitch McConnell  voted against the College Cost Reduction and Access Act.

Energy and the Environment Mitch McConnell  voted against the legislationt to end oil subsidies.

Gay Marriage Mitch McConnell  voted in favor of the 2006 amendment to define marriage as between one man and one woman.

Health Care Mitch McConnell  voted in favor of repealing the health care reform legislation.

Immigration Mitch McConnell  voted against removing the amendment, and therefore opposed the sanctuary city policy.

TARP Mitch McConnell  voted in favor of the Emergency Economic Stabilization Act.

Taxes Mitch McConnell  voted against extending the Bush Tax Cuts for all except the highest earners.

The Economy Mitch McConnell  voted against the Wall Street Reform Legislation.

The Second Amendment Mitch McConnell  voted in favor of the amendment to allow citizens to transport firearms on Amtrak.
