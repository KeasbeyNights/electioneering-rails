Abortion Daniel Akaka  voted against the amendment to prevent aid funds from being used for coercive abortions.

Education Daniel Akaka  voted in favor of the College Cost Reduction and Access Act.

Energy and the Environment Daniel Akaka  voted in favor of ending oil subsidies.

Gay Marriage Daniel Akaka  voted against the 2006 amendment to define marriage as between one man and one woman.

Health Care Daniel Akaka  voted against repealing the health care reform legislation.

Immigration Daniel Akaka  voted to table the amendment, and therefore supported the sanctuary city policy.

TARP Daniel Akaka  voted in favor of the Emergency Economic Stabilization Act.

Taxes Daniel Akaka  voted in favor extending the Bush Tax Cuts for all except the highest earners.

The Economy Daniel Akaka  voted in favor of the Wall Street Reform Legislation.

The Second Amendment Daniel Akaka  voted against the amendment to allow citizens to transport firearms on Amtrak.
